A Product Designer based in Seville, Spain

As a Designer, I am passionate about solving real problems and creating intuitive, functional and clear experiences.

I currently work as a remote Product Designer at Beezy, a digital intranet. My tasks range from bulding the foundations of a new Design System to designing new features for the product from research to implementation. I have also previously worked in the Design System Team in Liferay, building Lexicon.

In the past, I worked as a Primary School Teacher for 4 years. But my insatiable curiosity and love for new technologies led me to learn more about user centered design in the digital world. I enrolled at Ironhack's UX/UI Bootcamp in 2019 to take a professional leap towards a career that would allow me to grow and learn continuously.

While my primary focus lies with design, I try to consistently challenge myself in all areas and expand my knowledge in front-end development as well.

Besides the technological world, I have a passion for crossfit, yoga, cats and cooking. Food is my second language.
