Calquo

Calquo is a no-code calqculator form to help businesses generate leads to boost sales and engagement, reducing the hassle.

This was designed in a 2 days design challenge that was later coded by Daniel González Reina and Carlos Sánchez Serrano.

The goal of this challenge was to create an MVP product to be implented and coded later.

Design Challenge Results

The deliverables at the end of the challenge were:

  • Log in screen.
  • Dashboard with created calculators.
  • Templates to start creating a calculator.
  • Calqulator with different types of inputs: image, numbers, formula, text, sliders, date, email and choice.
  • Visualization of the form.
